A more ethical approach to advertising: 50% of ad revenue is donated to charity, and users are in control. Engaged users means more revenue for you.

== Description ==

Good-loop makes it easier to earn money from your site while also supporting charities that you care about. Set-up is easy: simply place the widget on your page.

More information is available at https://www.good-loop.com/how-it-works.

== Installation ==

The plugin does not require any special set-up: just install the plugin and place a "Good-loop ad unit" widget on your page.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory
    OR Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
2. Activate the plugin
3. Go to the Appearance->Widgets menu
4. Drag-and-drop a "Good-loop ad unit" (found under "Available Widgets") into one of the sidebars
5. The adunit should now be running happily on your page. For further information, including how to get paid, please refer to the Settings->Good-loop page


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How does it work? =

Every time a visitor to your site watches a fifteen second Good-loop advert, we give 50% of the ad revenue to charity. 
We charge our advertisers a premium rate, meaning that you don't lose out as a result of having ads on your site that do good!
